frequently asked questions

everything you need to know about working with gem fresh supplies

how does your pricing compare to the market?
our prices are competitive with market rates, but with key advantages: 1) stable pricing for regular customers (no daily fluctuations), 2) bulk order discounts, and 3) no hidden fees. most clients save 5-15% compared to inconsistent market purchases when factoring in reduced waste and staff time savings.
what's your delivery schedule?
we deliver 6 days a week (monday-saturday). most clients set a regular delivery day (e.g., every tuesday and friday), but we can accommodate any schedule that works for your business. delivery windows are available from 5am-11am or 2pm-6pm.
can i adjust my order after placing it?
yes! you can adjust quantities up to 24 hours before delivery through our app, phone, or whatsapp. we understand that needs change and we build flexibility into our system to accommodate last-minute changes when possible.
what if i'm not satisfied with the quality?
we stand behind our quality 100%. if any item doesn't meet our freshness standards, we'll credit your account immediately - no questions asked. this happens in less than 2% of deliveries, and we have processes to prevent repeat issues.
how do you handle payment?
we offer flexible payment terms: 1) pay per delivery (mpesa or bank transfer), 2) weekly billing, or 3) monthly invoicing for established clients. all payment methods are secure and documented with receipts.
